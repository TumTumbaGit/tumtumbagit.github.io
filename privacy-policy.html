This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile games built with Unreal Engine 5 and published on Google Play. By using our apps, you agree to this Policy.

We do not collect personally identifiable information (such as your name, address, or email) unless you voluntarily provide it when contacting us. Our apps and integrated services may automatically process:

We use Google AdMob to show banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads. AdMob may collect and process data to provide, measure, and improve ads, including for personalization where allowed by law and your device settings.

Learn more in Google’s policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings: Android → Settings → Google → Ads → “Opt out of Ads Personalization” (wording may vary by version).

3. In-App Purchases

Our games may offer premium features, cosmetic items (skins), and ad removal via Google Play Billing. Transactions are processed by Google; we do not receive or store your payment card details. We may store non-personal purchase metadata (e.g., product ID, entitlement state) to grant content you own.

Our apps are not directed to children under the age where consent is required by local law (e.g., under 13 in the U.S.). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child provided personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.
